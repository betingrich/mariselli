Features Of Mercedes

1. Autostatusview

Stay updated without manually opening your contacts' status updates. Mercedes views them for you automatically, saving time and effort.

2. Music Video Downloader

Leverage YouTube's API to download high-quality music and videos directly from the platform. Ideal for offline enjoyment.

3. Access to GPT

Get access to premium OpenAI ChatGPT services through the bot. Enjoy AI-powered conversations, assistance, and creative ideas.

4. Wallpaper Downloader

Search and download stunning 4K wallpapers to enhance the look of your devices with ease.

5. Image Downloader

Use advanced search tools to download any image you want from Bing or Google by simply providing a descriptive prompt.

6. Logo Maker

Design unique logos based on your prompts. Perfect for businesses, events, or personal branding.

7. Anime Content

Enjoy downloading anime images and videos with specific prompts, tailored to your favorite shows and characters.

8. Group Management

Remove all members, manage roles, and maintain order in your WhatsApp groups effortlessly using Mercedes.

9. Antilink

Ensure group security by detecting and removing unauthorized links. The bot also kicks the sender to maintain decorum.

10. Autosticker

Create fun and dynamic stickers on the go. Activate this feature to send stickers automatically during chats.

11. Anti Bad Word

Protect your group chats by automatically detecting and deleting messages containing inappropriate language.

12. Media Downloader

Seamlessly download media from MediaFire, Facebook, TikTok, Twitter, and Google Drive without watermarks. Ideal for quick sharing.

13. Scheduled Messages

Plan and automate your message delivery for birthdays, reminders, or group notifications.

14. Weather Updates

Get real-time weather forecasts directly on WhatsApp with accurate location-based information.

15. Currency Converter

Convert between currencies instantly. Perfect for travelers and international businesses.
